  • Water Flow Texture Maker

    In 4th year game dev, everyone was given the opportunity to program a custom shader design of their choice. I was interested in water effects, and the professor Wilf Lalonde wanted a quick program that would allow for a spatial vector representation of the direction the water should flow. The tool was useful, and gave me an A+ in the class, but its mostly interesting because it makes pretty pictures.

  • Century Club

    This little windows executable called to the need of party goers who want to play a drinking game called century club, and listen to music at the same time. When the application starts, you are prompted to point to your music directory. Then the timer starts and begins your playlist. Every 60 seconds, an audio cueue is played, and the next song is played.
